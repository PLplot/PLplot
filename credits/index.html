The Authors

The current version of PLplot is primarily a product of Maurice J. LeBrun and Geoffrey Furnish. The conversion of the documentation to DocBook form and subsequent upgrading of that documentation is primarily the product of Rafael Laboissiere and Alan W. Irwin. These four people and Joao Cardoso, Vince Darley, and Andrew Roach (the three other members of the core team) continue to commit documentation and code to the CVS.
